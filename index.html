High Performance Parallel Computing Workshop

About the Workshop

The primary goal of the workshop is to equip participants with a comprehensive understanding of High-Performance Computing (HPC) by focusing on parallel computing and Parallel Programming Models. Providing background on parallel computing, HPC fundamentals, including implementation strategies, programming paradigms, and real-world applications. In addition, participants will explore advanced topics including interconnection networks, NoC architecture, and parallel machine learning, combining theoretical knowledge with practical hands-on experience.
